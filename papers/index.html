A Unified Mathematical Model for Truth-Probability and Importance-Scoring of Statements
June 2026·working paper·epistemology / Bayesian modeling

A formal framework for scoring statements along three axes: T_abs(s), objective truth-probability; T_perc(s,r), perceived truth-probability for a receiver; and I(s,r), receiver-dependent importance. The model combines Bayesian updating, information theory, source credibility, statement properties, delivery context, receiver properties, and corroboration.
