About

I am Mohamed Amine Belachhab, a student from Fes, Morocco, applying for Fall 2027. My interests sit between applied mathematics, epistemology, economics, philosophy, political science, and AI safety.

I like questions that refuse to stay inside one department. Why do people believe things that are false? How should a belief update when evidence arrives? Can a mathematical model distinguish between what is true, what appears true, and what matters to a particular person? These questions have pulled me into Bayesian reasoning, information theory, political psychology, and formal epistemology.

I work independently, usually by building first and correcting later. And via this mindset I was able to do what I did, and present what I produced here.
