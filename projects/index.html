Creative work

This page collects selected creative and public-facing work: photography editions, calligrams, sketch booklets, YouTube projects, and teaching-related work. These projects were built independently, often with simple tools, and developed alongside my research as another way of turning observations, questions, and fragments into shareable work.

Public reading files are provided as PDFs. Some works are informal by design; they are included here because they represent sustained making, revision, and documentation outside school.

A documentary-style photo series built from ordinary scenes: meals, streets, school spaces, rural land, night scenes, and small moments of daily life. The images are mostly unedited, with timestamps kept as part of the record.
